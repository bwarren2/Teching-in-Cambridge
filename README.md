## General Advice

Get a bike share membership for the fair-weather months.  It makes all of Cambridge much more navigable.

Cambridge is a place built around institutions; universities, jobs, w/e.  Making social inroads to those groups is important for your long-term happiness.  Cambridge can feel a bit lonely otherwise.
